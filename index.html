Artist, Animator, Writer, Storyteller

Jack of Many Trades

As a student in my first year I was introduced to the primary Adobe pipeline in Illustrator, Photoshop and Animate.

From it I gained various skills surrounding content creation and management that I can transfer between programs and workflows.

I also took on the role of a tutor for this class as a second-year student, practising my skills by helping out the group of students I was responsible for.

In my second year of studies I was introduced to the basic workflow of Blender.

From it I gained a lot of skill in enviroment design, taking the time to learn concepts extending from our coursework to further improve my work.

I managed to attain a third place award in our class for my work in our semester assignment.

From this point I moved to practising character modelling in Blender, using references created by other artists to build their characters in a modelling enviroment. I gave myself the challenge to use what I was confident with in a completely new way.

I've also spent many hours creating artworks for myself, practising various aspects such as background work, character design, posing and creating works that others could enjoy and recognise.

Especially withing the past year I've pushed myself within character design to create visual representations of character's I've previously only imagined or saw through other's designs.

I also have a significant range of artwork made with the intention of using enviromental design to tell a story.
